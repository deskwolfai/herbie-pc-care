Terms & Conditions.

The shop rules, in plain words. By booking a service, dropping off a unit, or hiring us for home service, you agree to the following terms.

Effective · 1 January 2026 Last updated · 3 May 2026 Governing law · Philippines

1. Scope of services

Herbie PC Care provides cleaning, thermal repaste, fan service, cable management, and related maintenance for desktop computers, laptops, and similar consumer-grade computing hardware. We do not perform data recovery, software installation, operating-system reinstallation, or virus removal as part of our standard tiers. Such services may be referred or quoted separately.

2. The quote-first policy

Every unit is inspected and quoted before any disassembly begins. The price you are quoted at intake is the price you will pay at pickup, provided the actual condition of the unit matches what was visible at intake. If, during disassembly, we discover a hidden condition that materially changes the scope of work (for example, a damaged socket, a previously-failed repair, missing screws), we will pause work and contact you for approval before continuing.

3. Payment terms

  • Payment in full is due upon pickup or completion of home service.
  • Accepted payment methods: cash (PHP), GCash, and bank transfer to a nominated account. We do not accept post-dated cheques.
  • For Overhaul-tier services involving sourced parts, a 30% deposit may be required at intake to cover parts procurement.
  • Units left unclaimed for more than 60 days after notification of completion may be subject to storage fees and ultimately disposed of in accordance with Philippine consumer-protection law.

4. Customer responsibilities

Before handing your unit to us for service, you are responsible for:

  • Backing up your data. We strongly recommend backing up all important files, photographs, and documents before any service. While our work is on the hardware and not the data, hardware service inherently carries risk of unforeseen failure.
  • Removing accounts and passwords from active sessions if you have privacy concerns, although we never browse, open, or access user files.
  • Disclosing prior repair attempts at intake. Hidden previous repairs (improper paste application, damaged screws, replaced cooling solutions) significantly affect the time and risk of our work.
  • Removing peripherals and accessories not required for the service. We are not responsible for items left attached or stored inside the unit (USB drives, SD cards, paperwork).

5. Limitations of liability

Herbie PC Care will exercise professional care on every unit we service. However, the following limitations apply:

  • Data loss: We are not liable for loss of data resulting from hardware failure occurring during or after service. You are responsible for maintaining your own backups.
  • Pre-existing failures: We are not liable for component failures that were present, latent, or developing before your unit was brought to us. Hardware that was already on the verge of failing may complete that failure during service through no fault of ours.
  • Maximum liability: In any event, our total liability for any claim arising from a service is limited to the amount paid for that service.
  • Consequential damages: We are not liable for consequential, incidental, or indirect damages including but not limited to lost income, lost work product, or loss of opportunity.

6. Warranty

All standard tiers (Refresh, Deep Clean) include a 14-day workmanship warranty. If, within 14 days of pickup, the unit exhibits issues directly attributable to the work performed (a fan that wasn't reseated correctly, a cable that wasn't reconnected), we will re-service the unit at no additional charge.

The Overhaul tier includes an additional 30-day temperature warranty: if your CPU temperatures under standard load exceed our delivered baseline by more than 10°C within 30 days, we will diagnose and re-service the cooling system at no additional charge.

Warranties do not cover: damage from drops, spills, or misuse after pickup; failures of components we did not service; pre-existing conditions that were disclosed at intake; or units modified by third parties after our service.

7. Cancellation and rescheduling

Bookings may be cancelled or rescheduled at no charge up to 24 hours before the appointment. Cancellations within 24 hours of a scheduled home-service visit may incur a transportation fee equivalent to the originally-quoted travel cost.

8. Home service and transportation

Home service is available across Metro Manila for a minimal transportation fee that will be quoted with your service price before your appointment is confirmed. Home service is performed on-site only when feasible; some services (Overhaul, fan replacement) may require us to take the unit to the workshop and return it the following day.

9. Photographs and quality records

By engaging our service, you consent to us photographing your unit during disassembly and reassembly for internal quality control. These photographs are stored privately as described in our Privacy Policy and are never published or shared without your separate written consent.

10. Force majeure

We are not liable for delays or non-performance caused by events beyond our reasonable control, including but not limited to natural disasters, civil disturbance, government action, supplier failure, or extended power outages affecting the workshop.
